SUPERMAN

Sent as a baby to Earth from the dying planet Krypton, Kal-El was adopted by Martha and Jonathan Kent of Smallville, Kansas. Growing up as Clark Kent, he
devoted his life to helping others with the abilities he developed from Earth's sun. Moving to Metropolis, he became Superman, while still
maintaining his secret identity as Clark Kent, who works at the Daily Planet newspaper.
